Microplastics Detection & Analysis

Our iGEM team is developing innovative biosensors for real-time microplastic detection in environmental samples. Using synthetic biology approaches, we're creating engineered organisms capable of identifying and quantifying microplastic contamination in water sources, food products, and biological systems.
